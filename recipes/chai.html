Chai

Chai, also known as masala chai, is a warm and comforting Indian spiced tea. It's a perfect beverage to enjoy any time, especially on a relaxing day.

Now let's make a delightful cup of chai.

  • Ingredients
    1. Water
    2. Tea leaves or tea bags
    3. Milk
    4. Sugar (optional, adjust to taste)
    5. Whole spices (e.g., cardamom, cinnamon, cloves, ginger)
  • Let's Brew
    1. In a saucepan, bring water to a boil.
    2. Add tea leaves or tea bags to the boiling water.
    3. Crush or grate the whole spices (cardamom, cinnamon, cloves, ginger) and add them to the boiling tea.
    4. Let the tea simmer for a few minutes to infuse the flavors.
    5. Add milk to the tea. Adjust the quantity based on your preference for strength and creaminess.
    6. If desired, add sugar to taste. Stir well to dissolve the sugar.
    7. Simmer the chai for a few more minutes, allowing the flavors to meld.
    8. Strain the chai into cups and enjoy your warm, spiced tea.
  • Enjoy Your Chai Time!
